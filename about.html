ABOUT US

At We Walk Together we are a diverse group of Israelis and Americans who forged meaningful connections through the worlds of tourism and Zionist education. United by a shared love for our countries and a deep belief in the significance of our bond, we have come together with a common purpose: to protect, nurture, and celebrate the uniqueconnection between Israel and the United States.

Our mission is driven by a profound commitment to safeguarding this vital relationship amid the evolving challenges facing Zionism and traditional values. We believe that education is the cornerstone of understanding, and we strive to foster this understanding through various avenues. From organizing missions, seminars, and engaging with community leaders to spreading the truth and combating falsehoods, we work tirelessly to promote unity and awareness.

We also bridge cultural divides by facilitating cultural exchange and dialogue. By hosting talks in the United States and bringing visitors to Israel, we aim to create meaningful connections that transcend borders. We Walk Together is more than just an organization; it’s a movement dedicated to enlightening minds, nurturing cultural inclusivity, and celebrating the shared history and values that bind us all.


Our commitment extends to embracing the rich cultural heritage of African American and Latino communities in the United States. We actively collaborate with these communities to promote broader cultural engagement and understanding. Through these partnerships, we aim to build bridges between diverse traditions and create a tapestry of shared experiences.
