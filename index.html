THE REAL STORY

The history of Israel is a complex and contested one, spanning thousands of years and encompassing diverse populations, empires, and conflicts. It’s important to be aware of the various perspectives and narratives surrounding this region to understand its full story.

We educate people around the world about Israel

We stand up to misinformation and hate

We fight Antisemitism

ABOUT US

Welcome to 'We Walk Together', an organization deeply committed to creating meaningful connections across nations and cultures. Our mission is a testament to the power of unity, as we strive to bridge the gap between Americans and Israelis, Christians and Jews, and particularly focus on forging strong bonds with African American and Latino communities.
