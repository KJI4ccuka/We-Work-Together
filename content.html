Our Mission at We Walk Together

Welcome toWe Walk Together, an organization committed to creating meaningful connections across nations and cultures. Our mission is a testament to the power of unity, as we strive to bridge the gap between Americans and Israelis, Christians and Jews, and particularly focus on forging strong bonds with African American and Latino communities.

At the core of our mission is the belief that the friendship between the State of Israel and the United States harbors immense blessings and opportunities for mutual growth. We understand that true friendship and understanding are rooted in knowledge, empathy and education. In the realm of U.S.-Israel relations, our goal is to unlock the vast potential for learning and collaboration between the two nations.

WE WALK TOGETHER

is more than just an organization; it is a movement dedicated to education, enlightenment, and cultural integration. Our commitment extends to empowering and educating all who seek understanding, with special emphasis on our African American and Latino brothers and sisters. Our educational programs cover a broad spectrum, from the rich history of Israel to the dynamic and diverse tapestry of its people, the mosaic of religions within Israel’s borders, the challenges it faces, and the enriching relationship between the citizens of Israel the citizens of the United States.

As an independent non-governmental and non-government

We Walk Togetherfocuses on education and cultural exchange, free from political affiliations. We take pride in our patriotic values that resonate in both Israel and the United States, and we are dedicated to sharing these values with a diverse audience, including African American and Latino communities.

Join us at We Walk Together

as we embark on a journey of discovery, unity, and friendship. Together, we aim to build bridges of understanding, foster cultural inclusiveness, and shine a light on the shared history and values that connect us all , with a special focus on engaging and embracing the rich cultural heritage of the African American and Latino communities.
